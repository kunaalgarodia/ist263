About KTP Apparel

At KTP, we are more than just a community of tech enthusiasts – we are committed to making a difference. Our exclusive apparel line is designed to showcase our fraternity’s pride while providing high-quality, comfortable clothing for everyday wear.

Our Mission

By purchasing from KTP Apparel, you’re not just getting great merch – you're supporting a cause. A portion of every sale goes directly to The Museum of Science and Technology (MOST), an organization dedicated to informal science learning experiences that ignite curiosity, encourage discovery, and inspire investigation.

Why Buy From Us?

  • High-Quality Products: Each item is carefully selected for comfort, durability, and style, perfect for fraternity members and tech lovers alike.
  • Support a Cause: Every purchase contributes to meaningful change, helping the MOST continue its valuable work.
  • Fraternity Pride: Show your loyalty and pride with exclusive gear designed by and for our members.
